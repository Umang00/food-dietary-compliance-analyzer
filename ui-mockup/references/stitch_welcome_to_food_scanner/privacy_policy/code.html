Privacy Policy

schedule Last updated: Oct 24, 2023

We value your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your dietary data, allergy information, and scanning history to ensure your health and beliefs are respected and protected.

1 Data Collection

When you use our scanning feature, we process the barcode data to identify food products. We may also collect data regarding your specific dietary preferences (e.g., Vegan, Halal, Gluten-Free) solely to provide accurate suitability warnings. This data is stored locally on your device unless you opt-in for cloud backup.

2 Use of Information

Your information is used strictly to improve the accuracy of our food database and to personalize your experience. We do not sell your personal dietary habits to third-party advertisers.

Aggregated, anonymized data may be used for analytical purposes to understand general food trends.

3 Third-Party Services

Our app may contain links to third-party websites or services (such as nutritional databases) that are not owned or controlled by us. We advise you to review the privacy policies of any third-party sites or services that you visit.

4 Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.
