What is it?

Derived from dehydrated onions

Onion powder is a seasoning made from dehydrated ground onions. It provides a concentrated onion flavor and is commonly used as a seasoning in soups, stews, marinades, and spice rubs where fresh onion texture isn't desired.

warning

Why is it unsafe?

Onion powder is derived from onion, which is a root vegetable. Root vegetables are prohibited in a strict Jain diet because harvesting them kills the entire plant and involves potential harm to microorganisms in the soil.

Also known as

Dehydrated Onion Granulated Onion Ground Onion Allium Cepa Powder

Safe Alternatives

SAFE

Asafoetida (Hing)

Offers a similar savory, umami punch to cooked dishes without using root vegetables. Perfect for Jain cooking.
