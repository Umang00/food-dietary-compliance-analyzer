Instructions

1

Rinse the lentils

Wash the yellow moong dal thoroughly under running water until the water runs clear. Soak for 20 minutes to reduce cooking time.

2

Prepare the base

Heat ghee or oil in a pressure cooker. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter. Add ginger paste and chopped tomatoes. Saut√© for 2-3 minutes.

3

Cook the lentils

Add the drained lentils, turmeric, salt, and 3 cups of water. Pressure cook for 3 whistles or simmer in a pot for 25 minutes until soft.

4

Garnish and Serve

Open the cooker once pressure releases. Mash the dal slightly for a creamy texture. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot.
